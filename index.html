Docs

Documentation, often abbreviated as "docs," refers to written or visual materials that provide information, instructions, or explanations about a particular subject, product, or process. Documentation can take various forms, including manuals, guides, tutorials, FAQs, and reference materials. It serves as a valuable resource for users, developers, and stakeholders to understand how to use a product, troubleshoot issues, or learn about its features and functionalities. Good documentation is clear, concise, and well-organized, making it easier for users to find the information they need and effectively utilize the product or service.
